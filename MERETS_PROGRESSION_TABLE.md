## Formula

**Merets Required:**
- Levels 1-10: 1 meret per level
- Levels 11-20: 2 merets per level
- Levels 21-30: 4 merets per level
- Levels 31+: Each level requires 1.06× more merets than the previous level

**Merets Earned Per Task:**
```
merets_earned = (effort_minutes / 60) × quality_multiplier

Quality Multipliers:
- 5 stars: 1.20×
- 4 stars: 1.00×
- 3 stars: 0.70×
- 2 stars: 0.40×
- 1 star:  0.20×
```

**Example:**
- 90-minute task with 5-star rating: (90/60) × 1.20 = 1.80 merets
- 60-minute task with 4-star rating: (60/60) × 1.00 = 1.00 merets
- 30-minute task with 3-star rating: (30/60) × 0.70 = 0.35 merets
